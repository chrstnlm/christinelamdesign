Sandwich coasters
2018
Sandwich coasters are made out of intricate and playful forms. When they are all stack on top of one another, they create a slight “trompe-oeil” illusion effect. In this case, each coaster represent different pieces of food (tomato, cheese, lettuce, onion, ham, toasts) and when they are together, it looks like a sandwich. My main idea was to take an everyday object and make it fun and attractive by playing with different shapes, forms and colours. The product can be used by anyone – from kids to elderly people.
Materials
Plywood
Acrylic paint
