Play With Your Food
2019
A card game that explores and questions the relationships that we - human beings - have with food and ingredients that we consume on our daily basis, as not everyone has the same experience. The game was created with the intention to create conversations around the table during lunch or dinner time and invites the players to “play” with the food that is in front of them.
