Voltable
2018
Collaborative work with
Antoinette Handal
We chose volcano because of its aesthetic. We see volcanos more as a beautiful aspect of nature, instead of something aggressive or scary. We were first inspired by the Santa Ana volcano in El Salvador but through our research, we were drawn by the its texture, shape, lava and other organic aspects of the volcano.
