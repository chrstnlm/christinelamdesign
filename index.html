Hello! Bonjour!

My name is Christine and I’m an illustrator and designer based in Montréal, Québec. I recently graduated from Concordia University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree, majoring in Design. I’m passionate about anything related to graphic design, digital arts, ceramics, printmaking and photography. In my free time, I enjoy learning new crafts and running my small business on Etsy.

As a designer, I strive to be more thoughtful about the decisions that I make concerning not only the materials used but also the ideas when it comes to the conception of any kind of project. Because design is political and has the power to define the things around us, I can feel the responsibility and the impact my actions and ideas can have not only on the environment but also on our society. I also believe that I can use design as a tool to make a difference, by helping those who are in need or left behind and make their voices known.
